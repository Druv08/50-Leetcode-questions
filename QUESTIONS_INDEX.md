## Study Tips

1. **Start with Easy**: Build confidence and understand basic patterns
2. **Progress to Medium**: Most interview questions fall in this category
3. **Master Hard**: Required for senior positions and competitive offers
4. **Understand Patterns**: Many problems use similar approaches
5. **Practice Daily**: Consistency is key to success
